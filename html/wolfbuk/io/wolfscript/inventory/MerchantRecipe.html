MerchantRecipe class

io.wolfscript.inventory.MerchantRecipe Implements Recipe

Class Overview

Represents a Villager's trade. Trades can take one or two ingredients, and provide one result. The ingredients' Itemstack amounts are respected in the trade.
A trade has a limited number of uses, after which the trade can no longer be used, unless the player uses a different trade, which will cause its maximum uses to increase.
A trade may or may not reward experience for being completed.
